Contours of Schuster's p-value

This plots represent the value of the Schuster's p-value for a set of earthquakes with magnitude (Ml) and depth lower than or equal to a given value. Thus, the point Ml=4.0 and depth 50 km in the plot correspond to the p-value calculated for all earthquakes with magnitude below or equal to 4.0 and depths lower or equal to 50 km.
